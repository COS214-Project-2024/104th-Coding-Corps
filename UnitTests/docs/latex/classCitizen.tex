Constructs a \mbox{\hyperlink{classCitizen}{Citizen}} with a unique ID, initial education level, and assigned job and residence. 

Initializes a citizen with attributes such as class type, job type, employment status, and financial metrics. The citizen is assigned a job based on their education level and a residence based on their class type, with satisfaction and standard of living adjusted accordingly.


\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em city\+Context} & Shared pointer to the \mbox{\hyperlink{classCityContext}{City\+Context}} for access to city-\/wide resources and citizen registration. \\
\hline
{\em transport\+Context} & Shared pointer to the \mbox{\hyperlink{classTransport}{Transport}} system for managing citizen travel between locations. \\
\hline
{\em government} & Shared pointer to the \mbox{\hyperlink{classGovernment}{Government}} for administrative access and regulatory updates.\\
\hline
\end{DoxyParams}
Initializes\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\ttfamily citizen\+ID}\+: Unique identifier for the citizen.
\item {\ttfamily satisfaction}, {\ttfamily expected\+Standard\+Of\+Living}\+: Initial satisfaction and expected living standards.
\item {\ttfamily class\+Type}\+: Randomly assigned social class (upper, middle, lower) influencing job and residence type.
\item {\ttfamily job\+Type}\+: Job role selected from a set available to the citizen\textquotesingle{}s education level.
\item {\ttfamily employed}\+: Employment status, determined randomly, with financial implications.
\item {\ttfamily residence}\+: Assigns nearest building based on class; adjusts satisfaction if no suitable residence is found.
\end{DoxyItemize}

Attaches the citizen to the {\ttfamily city\+Context} upon creation.
